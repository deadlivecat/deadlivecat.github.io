Escape from COVID19!

Escape from Covid-19 is a game about our exacerbated need to go out when it is forbidden or not recommended. After several days of lockdown, the negation and projection start to act at collective level. The excuses to go out are stacking and the reasons to stay inside are less and less important. So do it, go out and get Covid-19. If you survive, don't forget to donate plasma.

Rules are simple:

  • Stay away from other people
  • Gel alcohol will help you, but is not a silver bullet.
  • Police will send you back home unless you have a permit
  • Chlorine Dioxide may help (no, it doesn't)

    • Important note: Neither the Chlorine Dioxide (CDS) or Hydroxychloroquine (HQCD) are proven effective treatment against COVID-19. Moreover, the Chlorine Dioxide is a dangerous substance with proven nocive effects for health. We discourage the consumption of Chlorine Dioxide both in the game and in real life. Hydroxychloroquine should only be taken under medical supervision.
